NETFOX — netfox.noray + netfox.internals (Tamas Galffy and contributors)
  The noray NAT-punchthrough / relay join flow for the co-op lobby
  codes (MULTIPLAYER.md par.3.2): noray client, UDP packet handshake,
  protocol parser, plus the netfox logger the noray addon depends on
  (netfox.internals). The rest of netfox (core / extras / rollback)
  is NOT vendored — NetworkManager (scripts/net/net_manager.gd) talks
  to these addons directly.

  Source:  https://github.com/foxssake/netfox
  Version: v1.35.3 (tag), commit c7fc6ea52b4e9087c980d9ca1f8c3826e3a6caa4
  License: MIT — Copyright 2023 Gálffy Tamás
           (full text: netfox.noray/LICENSE, netfox.internals/LICENSE)

  Vendoring rules: upstream file layout is kept verbatim (including
  .uid files) so a future update is a clean diff against the same two
  addon folders at a newer tag. Do not edit vendored files — wrap or
  extend from scripts/net/ instead. The addons' EditorPlugins are NOT
  enabled in project.godot (we don't want their Noray/PacketHandshake
  autoloads or editor settings); NetworkManager instantiates the
  scripts it needs itself.
