Music override folder
=====================
Drop a .ogg, .mp3 or .wav file here named after a track to replace it,
looped automatically. Track names: village, darkwood, marsh, keep,
magma, icefield, graveyard, desert, rainstorm, holy, crystalline,
boss_fangmaw, boss_morwen, boss_vargoth.
Delete a file to fall back to the built-in composed version.

Current tracks (all from OpenGameArt.org):
- magma.ogg       "Lava Area Theme" by Wolfgang_ — CC-BY 4.0
                  https://opengameart.org/content/lava-area-theme
- icefield.ogg    "Snow May Never End (v2)" by Sindwiller — CC-BY-SA 3.0
                  https://opengameart.org/content/snow-may-never-end
- graveyard.ogg   "RPG - The Graveyard" by HitCtrl — CC-BY 3.0
                  https://opengameart.org/content/rpg-the-graveyard
- desert.mp3      "Nomads" by elerya — CC-BY 3.0
                  https://opengameart.org/content/nomads
- rainstorm.ogg   "rain and thunders" by kindland — CC0
                  https://opengameart.org/content/rain-and-thunders
- holy.wav        "Cathedral" by Umplix — CC0
                  https://opengameart.org/content/cathedral-0
- crystalline.mp3 "Crystal Cave (song18)" by cynicmusic (pixelsphere.org) — CC0
                  https://opengameart.org/content/crystal-cave-song18

CC-BY tracks require the above attribution to ship with the game.
village / darkwood / marsh / keep and the three boss themes are
original compositions synthesized in scripts/music.gd.
