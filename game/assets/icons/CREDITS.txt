How overrides work: a 32x32 PNG here named after an Art.GEAR_SHAPES
shape (e.g. w_blade.png, icon_sigil.png) replaces the procedural gear
icon, untinted — grade rarity is carried by bag-slot border colors and
item-name colors. potion.png overrides the HUD potion icon (Art.tex).
