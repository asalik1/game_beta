Combat FX + ambient water sprites in this folder are cropped/recolored
frames from the "Ninja Adventure - Asset Pack" by pixel-boy (Pierre Vigier)
and contributors.

Processing applied by us: cropped from the pack's FX / animated-background
sprite sheets, per-channel RGB gamma-brightened (~0.80) to survive the
Forward+ HDR tonemap, alpha preserved. Frames left as horizontal strips and
animated at runtime (Sprite2D hframes).
