Legacy + backup asset reuse gallery

Actual sprite review for unassigned art. Overall priority weighs aesthetics, role clarity, and implementation completeness. “Complete” means usable without generating new sprite work; non-directional strips are valid for normal enemy placeholders.

8.0–10: strongest next-use candidates 6.0–7.9: good reserve / needs a content home under 6: niche or only if intentionally stylized Source links open the actual local asset
